Construction and Design Agreements

The preparation and negotiation of AIA (and other forms) of construction contracts, architect’s agreements, engineer’s agreements, project management agreements, equipment acquisition agreements, payment and performance bonds, construction loan agreements and related documentation, including in connection with the development of a co-generation power plant, leases of office space, renovations of office space, the renovation of a hotel, the development of luxury residences.
